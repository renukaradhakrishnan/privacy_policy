Privacy Policy & Data Usage

Welcome to Pinsight. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a comprehensive psychiatric educational platform. This policy explains how we handle data for both our User (Student) and Admin modules.

1. Information We Collect

A. User (Student) Data

When you register as a User to take assessments, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, Email address, and secure Password for login verification.
  • Assessment Data: Your responses to Pre-tests and Post-test quizzes.
  • Progress Tracking: Data regarding which video modules you have watched and your completion status.
  • Performance Results: Your calculated scores on psychiatry quizzes to generate your Result Page.

B. Admin Data

When you register as an Administrator, we collect:

  • Credentials: Login details and Password change verification logs.
  • Content Management: Logs of questions edited, videos uploaded, or content modified within the system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity when logging in as either a User or Admin.
  • Educational Analysis: To compare your Pre-test and Post-test results and display your learning improvement.
  • Admin Analytics: To provide Administrators with aggregate data, such as the total number of users and comparative performance statistics.
  • System Improvement: To allow Admins to edit questions and videos based on user performance data.

3. Data Sharing & Security

Admin Access: Authorized Administrators have access to aggregate user statistics and comparison data to improve the educational content.

Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your password and personal information. Passwords are encrypted during verification.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

As a user of Pinsight, you have the right to:

  • Access your profile and viewing history.
  • Request corrections to your personal data.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated quiz history.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or the content within the Pinsight app, please contact our support team.
